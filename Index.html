Insurance Producer

Take your sales career to the next level by helping clients protect what matters most to them with customized insurance solutions.

Responsibilities:

  • Generate new business through prospecting, networking, and referrals
  • Conduct personalized insurance needs analyses for clients
  • Develop and present customized insurance solutions
  • Build and maintain long-term client relationships
  • Meet or exceed monthly sales goals
  • Stay current on industry trends, products, and regulations

Qualifications:

  • Current state insurance license or ability to obtain one within 90 days
  • Sales experience preferred but not required
  • Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
  • Self-motivated with a strong work ethic
  • Ability to build trust and relationships quickly
  • Passion for helping others and solving problems

Benefits:

  • Competitive commission structure with uncapped earning potential
  • Performance bonuses and incentives
  • Comprehensive training and development
  • Leads and marketing support
  • Advanced technology platform

Appointment Setter

Drive the sales process by connecting potential clients with our insurance producers through effective phone and digital communication.

Responsibilities:

  • Contact potential clients through outbound calling and digital platforms
  • Qualify leads based on specific criteria
  • Schedule appointments for insurance producers
  • Maintain detailed records of calls and contacts
  • Achieve daily and weekly appointment setting goals
  • Provide excellent customer service in all interactions

Qualifications:

  • Previous call center or telemarketing experience preferred
  • Excellent phone presence and communication skills
  • Persuasive with a positive attitude
  • Goal-oriented and resilient
  • Basic computer skills
  • Ability to follow scripts while maintaining natural conversation

Benefits:

  • Competitive base pay plus appointment bonuses
  • Opportunity to advance to producer role
  • Comprehensive training program
  • Supportive team environment
  • Flexible scheduling options
Full-time/Part-time Base + Bonuses Remote Available
